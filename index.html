Core Principles

What ATLAS will never do

Three non-negotiable commitments baked into the architecture, not just the policy.

🚫

Never Rank Students

ATLAS identifies needs and matches resources. It never produces student rankings, comparison scores, or labels that follow students across contexts.

🤝

Never Replace Teachers

Every recommendation requires human approval. ATLAS proposes — educators decide. The system augments professional judgment, never overrides it.

🔒

Never Sell Data

Student data belongs to students and districts. No advertising, no third-party sales, no secondary uses. Privacy is structural, not just a policy.
