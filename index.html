Trust & Safety

Three commitments baked into
the architecture itself.

Not just policies. These are structural constraints enforced by code.

🚫

Never Rank Students

ATLAS identifies needs and matches resources. It never produces student rankings or comparison scores. Every child is evaluated against their own trajectory, not their peers.

🤝

Humans Always Decide

Every recommendation requires human approval before action. ATLAS proposes and explains its reasoning — educators decide. The system augments judgment, never replaces it.

🔒

Privacy Is Structural

FERPA and COPPA compliance built into the data model, not bolted on. AES-256 encryption, crypto-shredding for data deletion, and role-based access at every layer. No data sales. Ever.
