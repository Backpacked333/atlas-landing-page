Security & Roadmap

FERPA-compliant by design, built to scale

FERPA/COPPA-compliant security architecture with ABAC+ReBAC access control, per-district encryption, and crypto-shredding. Plus a 30-week, 6-phase implementation plan from foundation to full autonomy.

FERPA/COPPA ABAC + ReBAC 6 Phases 30 Weeks

FERPA

Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act

  • Legitimate educational interest enforcement
  • Directory information vs. PII separation
  • Parent/guardian access rights implementation
  • Annual notification compliance tracking
  • Audit trail for every record access

COPPA

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act

  • Verifiable parental consent workflows
  • Data minimization for under-13 learners
  • No behavioral advertising or profiling
  • Right to review and delete child data
  • Third-party vendor consent management

State Laws

State-specific student privacy regulations

  • Configurable per-state policy engine
  • Data residency requirements by jurisdiction
  • Breach notification timeline compliance
  • Vendor agreement template management
  • Annual security assessment scheduling
ABAC + ReBAC — dual-layer authorization

Attribute-Based Access Control defines what you can see based on your role and attributes. Relationship-Based Access Control refines it based on your actual relationships to students and schools. Both gates must pass.

Encryption & crypto-shredding — per-district keys

Per-district encryption keys with hardware-backed key management. When a district leaves, crypto-shredding ensures complete, verifiable data destruction.

Immutable audit trail — logging & tamper evidence

Every data access, model prediction, and recommendation is logged with full provenance. Tamper-evident logs support compliance audits and incident investigation.

Tamper Evidence

Log entries are hash-chained (SHA-256) with periodic Merkle root publication. Any modification breaks the chain, making tampering immediately detectable.

Real-Time Alerting

Anomalous access patterns (bulk exports, off-hours access, unusual query scope) trigger real-time alerts to security team via PagerDuty.

Defense in depth — 7-layer security stack

Seven layers of security controls from network perimeter to application logic. All layers evaluated on every request. Deny-by-default at each boundary.

30-week implementation roadmap — 6 phases

A phased rollout from infrastructure foundation to full autonomous operation, with clear gates between each phase.

Cross-functional delivery teams — 6 squads

Four specialized squads aligned to the architecture tiers, plus cross-cutting security and data science functions.

Platform Squad

Infrastructure, Kubernetes, CI/CD, observability, database administration. Owns the foundation and ingestion tiers.

Intelligence Squad

ML engineers, data scientists, LLM specialists. Owns feature engineering, risk models, LangGraph agents, and optimization solvers.

Product Squad

Frontend engineers, UX designers, product managers. Owns dashboards, educator workflows, and persona-specific interfaces.

Security & Compliance

Security engineers, compliance officers. Own ABAC/ReBAC policies, encryption, audit systems, and regulatory filings.

Data Engineering

Pipeline engineers, ETL specialists. Own source connectors, entity resolution, knowledge graph maintenance, and data quality.

Education Domain

Former educators, curriculum specialists, psychometricians. Own ontology design, intervention catalog curation, and causal study design.
